GUT HEALTH PROTOCOL
Built into every day — not an afterthought
🦠
Daily Probiotics
Plain Greek yogurt every day. Kefir from Week 3. These introduce good bacteria that reduce bloating and improve digestion.
🌾
High Fiber (30–35g)
Oats, black beans, chickpeas, broccoli, baby spinach daily. High fiber feeds the gut microbiome and keeps you full longer.
🧄
Prebiotics Daily
Garlic, onions, bananas, oats — these feed your good bacteria. Add garlic to every savory meal you cook.
🍵
Morning Ritual
Warm lemon water on waking. Ginger or chamomile tea at night. Simple free habit that makes a real difference.
🌶
Anti-Inflammatory Spices
Turmeric + black pepper daily. Add to eggs, rice, yogurt. Reduces inflammation that blocks fat loss.
😴
Sleep 8 Hours
Gut heals during sleep. Poor sleep spikes cortisol, increases hunger hormones, and destroys fat loss progress.
KEY SUCCESS RULES
Non-negotiables for hitting your target
01
Protein at Every Meal
Never eat without a protein source — eggs, chicken, Greek yogurt, beans. Aim for 35–45g per meal. This preserves muscle while losing fat.
02
Sunday Meal Prep
Batch-cook 3 lbs chicken, a pot of rice, boil a dozen eggs, roast veggies. Done in 2 hours. Feeds you the whole week.
03
Progressive Overload
Add weight to major lifts every 1–2 weeks. This tells your body to keep muscle while burning fat. Without this, you lose both.
04
Don't Skip Rest Days
Muscles grow and fat burns during recovery. Sleep 7–9 hrs. Active rest beats sitting — but full rest days are required.
05
Track, Don't Obsess
Weigh once a week, same morning, same conditions. Log daily in the Tracker. Weeks 3–4 are where real results start — trust it.
06
3.5L Water Daily
Dehydration kills performance, increases hunger, and slows metabolism. Drink water before every meal. Use the tracker.
